Privacy Policy for Kala-Kala

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Kala-Kala ("the app") does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us or any third party.

Data Collection

The app does not require an account, login, or registration. The app does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or location.

Local Storage

Any data you create within the app (such as saved color palettes or settings) is stored only on your device. We have no access to this data, and it is never sent to our servers because we do not operate any servers that receive app data.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13, because it does not collect data at all.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, an updated version will be posted at this same URL with a revised date above.
